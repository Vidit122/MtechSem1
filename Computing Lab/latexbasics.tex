\textbf{\section*{Mathematical Equations}
	These are some simple maths equations:
	\newline
	1. Pythagorus theorem: $a^2 + b^2 = c^2 $
	\newline
	2. Area of a triangle $= (b \times h) \div 2$
	\newline
	3. Basic Differential Equation: $\frac{d^2f(x)}{dx^2} = \frac{d^2(x^2 +53x + 1)}{dx^2} $
	\newline
	4. Integration Equation: $\int f(x) = \int x^4 +3x^3 + 5x^2 + 23x + 3$
	\newline
	5. Sine-cos: $ \sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1$
	\newline
	6. Integration over a range:  $\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} e^{x^2} \, dx $
	\newline
	7. Matrix: $ \begin{pmatrix}
		12 & 5 & 6 \\
		10 & 4 & 3 \\
	\end{pmatrix} $
	\newline
	8. Area of a Circle = $\pi \times r^2$
	\newline
	9. Factorial: $n! = n \times (n-1) \times (n-2)\times .....2 \times 1$
	\newline
	10. Union: $A \cup B = A + B - A \cap B$
	\newline
	11. Summation: $\sum_{i=0}^{n} = 0+ x1+x2+....xn$}
